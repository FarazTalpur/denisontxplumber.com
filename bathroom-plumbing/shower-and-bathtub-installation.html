Frequently Asked Questions

The average cost of installing a new shower can range from $1,200 to $5,800, while a bathtub installation typically ranges from $1,400 to $7,000, depending on the materials and complexity of the job.

Installation of a new shower or bathtub usually takes one to three days, although this can vary depending on the complexity of the project and unforeseen plumbing issues.

Typically, the old shower or bathtub will need to be removed before installing a new one. Most professional installers include the removal and disposal of the old fixture as part of their service.

While some experienced DIYers may be able to handle installation, it is generally recommended to hire a professional plumber or contractor to ensure the installation is done correctly and safely, especially for complex projects.

When selecting a new shower or bathtub, consider factors like size, shape, materials, features (e.g., jets, lighting), your bathroom layout, and compatibility with existing plumbing to ensure the best fit and function.
